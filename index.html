Documentary: The Resistance (working title)

We are passionate liberals seeking to chronicle the efforts of the "resistance"-- those grassroots movements daring to challenge the Trump administration and the Republican party. From women's rights to gun control, our job the next four years is to film what the media won't and tell the story of the Majority without political parties or pundits creating all that noise.

About

Natalie Ganoff

Director Natalie Ganoff moonlights as a compliance attorney that counsels the financial services industry on Dodd-Frank and the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau. She most fiercely wants to protect and expand women's rights and environmental justice.

Demetri Ganoff

Producer Demetri Ganoff is a techie that started coding when he was fifteen. Since then, he has been changing the internet, one website/web app at a time. He now wants to change the world by ensuring the film covers the movement's efforts on healthcare and gun control.
